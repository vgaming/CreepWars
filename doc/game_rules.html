Gold You get gold for each kill. First #gold_kills_to_increase kills give #gold_start_p0 gold, next #gold_kills_to_increase kills give #gold_start_p1 gold, next #gold_kills_to_increase kills kills give #gold_start_p2 gold, etc. Killing Leader counts as 4 creep kills. On 3p FFA map, killing Guard counts as #gold_guard_multiplier leader kills. Shop You cannot recruit, but you can spend gold in Shop to buy upgrades. Visiting Shop will also heal you. Score The cost of new AI creeps is equal to your score. You increase score with each kill. Only the number of killed units counts (leader kill counts same as creep kill). Additionally, if your score is below #lvl0_barrier, then only lvl0 units will be generated, and if your score is above #lvl3plus_barrier, you get random lvl3 unit. If your score is much higher than unit cost, the unit will have additional ''boost''. Other Guard health increases by 1 when killing a creep and by 3 when killing a leader. The Scoreboard has the following format: ''score gold (total_kills leader_kills)''.
